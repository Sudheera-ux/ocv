who_i_am

Hi, I'm Osanda — an undergraduate studying Cybersecurity, drawn to the discipline somewhere between offense and defence: understanding how attackers think in order to design systems that resist them.

My day-to-day sits across network security, secure software development, and digital forensics. I like problems with a clear adversary — a misconfigured firewall, a vulnerable endpoint, an exploit that shouldn't exist — and the satisfaction of closing the gap.

Outside coursework, I build small security tools, contribute to CTF write-ups, and keep a lab environment running for testing things I'm not supposed to test on production.
